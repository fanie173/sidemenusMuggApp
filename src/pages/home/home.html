We know that coffee is the only thing keeping every day from feeling like a Monday.
Even Mondays. That’s why waiting for it in long queues is not helping world peace.
So don’t. sMugg is a brand new app for your Android or iOS device that helps you skip the queues and get straight to the java.
And by “skipping the queues” we mean you don’t even have to leave your car,
the coffee comes to you. Now that’s coffee to go!
So how does sMugg work? The app uses GPS technology to help you find and place your coffee order at a network of partner coffee shops.
You can then arrange a pick-up time and a runner will meet you curb-side with your order ready to go.
No need to get out of your car. Cappuccino? Sugar? No milk? sMugg comes with a range of features to help you get your order just right.
You can even cancel or delay your pick-up time when you’re stuck in traffic, save your favourite coffee shops and orders for future use,
and it allows your location to be tracked by coffee shops so they know when to get your order ready.
